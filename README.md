# 🌟 KVG Trust Platform

> A production-ready full-stack NGO Management Platform built to streamline volunteer management, donations, event management, impact tracking, and digital operations for K V G Shanmukh Sai Trust.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The KVG Trust Platform is a comprehensive web application developed to digitize and simplify the operations of a charitable organization. It enables administrators, volunteers, donors, and members to interact through a secure and user-friendly platform while providing transparency and efficient management of trust activities.
